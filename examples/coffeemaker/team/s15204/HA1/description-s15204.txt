Use case - Purchase a beverage

Description:
Step | Action
 1   | User approaches the coffee maker
 2   | User puts enough money for a beverage of choice
 3   | User chooses a beverage
 4   | Coffee maker prepares a cup
 5   | Coffee maker adds all ingredients
 6   | (Optional) Coffee maker returns change
 7   | Coffee maker makes a sound, indicating the finish of coffee making
 8   | User retrieves the cup
